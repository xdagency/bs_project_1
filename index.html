Sound

Lyrics

Atomic level songwriting that makes noses run.

The semicolons join us from Moosejaw. The band has a soft sound and a soapy style, bringing infinite guitars, deep rhythm, sober vocals, and atomic songwriting to the table. The band has been running noses in venues across the world since 1984. The semicolons plays tunes from a variety of genres, and their influences include bands like NSYNC and Linkin Park. Look out for The SemiColons coming to your local Scarborough Town Centre this Summer!
